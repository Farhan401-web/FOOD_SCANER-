About NutriScan

How It Works

NutriScan uses the Open Food Facts database to provide detailed nutritional information. Simply scan a food item with your camera, search by name, or enter a barcode to get instant access to calories, macronutrients, vitamins, and more.

Data Sources

All nutritional data is sourced from Open Food Facts, a collaborative database containing information on over 2 million food products from around the world.

Privacy First

Your scan history is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit any personal data. All processing happens directly in your browser.
